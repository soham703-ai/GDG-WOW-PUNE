## 📋 About GDG WOW Pune

GDG WOW (Women of Web) Pune is a community-driven initiative focused on empowering women in technology through the Google Developers Group platform. We aim to create an inclusive environment where women developers can learn, connect, and grow together in their tech careers.

The WOW program is designed to support women's professional development in the web technology space through workshops, mentoring sessions, hackathons, and networking events.

## 🎯 Our Mission

- Foster diversity and inclusion in the tech community
- Provide learning and growth opportunities for women developers
- Create a supportive network for knowledge sharing and collaboration
- Showcase and celebrate women's achievements in technology
- Bridge the gender gap in the tech industry
